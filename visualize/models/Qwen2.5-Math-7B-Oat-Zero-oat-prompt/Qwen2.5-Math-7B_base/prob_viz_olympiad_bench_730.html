Since the trains pass Linh every 10 minutes (or \( \frac{10}{60} = \frac{1}{6} \) hours), the distance between consecutive trains (which is the distance Linh travels in 3 minutes, since the trains depart every 3 minutes) is the relative speed multiplied by the time interval between the trains passing Linh.
